HTML tags cheat sheet

Headings

Paragraphs of text go in 'h1' to 'h6' identify blocks and sections. Every page must have at least 1 h1 tag

Paragraphs

Paragraphs of text go in 'p' tags.

List

Unordered Lists

Unordered list are marked with bullet points and go in the 'ul' tags. Each list item goes in the li tags
